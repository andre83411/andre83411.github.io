About Me

I am very excited to have begun my journey as a program/web developer. This is a new path for me, and I consider it an adventure. I am glad to share my journey with you.

My previous work experience includes administrative and clerical roles, with the longest stint in the accounting world. The type of work environment that I enjoy the most is an ever changing, collaborative one. My strengths are problem solving, and learning new skills. I also enjoy training others once I master new skills.

I started dabbling in coding a while ago and quickly decided this is my path. I am a bit regretful I didn’t find it sooner! The constant demand to learn new things in this field, is what most attracted me to coding.

I am currently enrolled in the MIT Full Stack Developer program. This is the first Web Project within the bootcamp, and it is designed to showcase what I have learned so far. I really enjoyed putting together this project and look forward to the coming challenges.
